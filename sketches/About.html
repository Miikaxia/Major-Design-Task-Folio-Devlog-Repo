About Sketches

How to find sketches

When looking for sketches in this repository, they will likely be linked in this page, but also be available in the folders on the actual repository site at this link.
Furthermore, they will be named and linked in chronological order, using a simple naming scheme of sketch[number][additional notes] where the number is simply the sketch number, starting from 1, and the additional notes are a summary of relevant information. (Example: "sketch_2_final-design" or "sketch_3_improved").
